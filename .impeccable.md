## Design Context

### Users
Primary audience is people evaluating Nikita quickly: recruiters, hiring managers, engineering peers, and potential collaborators. Secondary audience is photography viewers who want a lightweight way to discover and contact him.

### Brand Personality
Calm, precise, and human. The site should feel competent and intentional without feeling corporate or over-designed.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined minimalism with editorial typography, generous whitespace, and subtle color tinting. Keep the structure simple (single-page profile) while elevating details such as rhythm, contrast, and interaction states.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with clarity: identity, role, and contact path visible immediately.
2. Keep cognitive load low: minimal sections, concise copy, no decorative clutter.
3. Prioritize craft: strong type hierarchy, spacing rhythm, and accessible interactions.
4. Preserve speed: static-first, small asset footprint, no unnecessary scripts.
5. Stay future-ready: easy to attach custom domain, expand sections, and update content.
